Pump:

Mostly AC submersible pumps with different HP ratings are used for irrigation purpose. A submersible pump is immersed in water. These pumps are designed for high head and medium flow application. Submersible pumps are installed completely underwater, including the motor.

Pump Controller:

An electronic device which matches the PV power to motor parameter & regulates the operations. It can be used on any AC 3 Ø pump, like Submersible Pump of given HP rating. Pump Controller is designed with MPPT technology and VFD to maximize water delivery at various solar energy levels. It has arrangement to accept 3 Ø ac input from grid so that you can run pump at full speed whenever solar energy is not sufficient.

  • The MPPT converts PV power into high-current and low-voltage power which satisfies the pump motor characteristics. And therefore maximizes the system efficiency.
  • VFD allows operating motors & pumps at the precise speed needed for your process. With the ability to vary motor and pump speed, system efficiency is increased.
PV Array:

An array of PV modules connected in series & possibly strings of modules connected in parallel.A number of PV modules are combined as an array to meet different energy demands.
